Common Questions

Answers before you ask.

What does the pilot include?

Everything. We integrate your data sources (CSVs, PDFs, spreadsheets, APIs, databases), configure role-based access, deploy the full stack, and give you an observability dashboard. For free. For 3 months.

What data formats does Kratu support?

PDF documents, Excel/CSV files, Firestore databases, PostgreSQL/MySQL, REST APIs, Google Drive folders, and SharePoint. If your data exists somewhere, we can index it.

Does my data leave my organisation?

No. Kratu can deploy to your own cloud or on-premise infrastructure. For cloud deployments, your data stays in a dedicated environment — never shared, never used for training.

How long does setup actually take?

Day 1: Data connection. Day 2: Indexing and embeddings. Day 3: Role configuration. Day 4: Deployment. Day 5: Your team is live. Five days is accurate. Often faster.

What happens after the pilot?

If you see value (and you will), the Institution plan is ₹29,999/month for unlimited users. If not, we help you cleanly transition out. No pressure, no lock-in.

What if my IT team has concerns?

We have an architecture deck, a security whitepaper, and we're happy to join a call with your IT team. Every answer in Kratu is cited, logged, and auditable. That's a feature, not an afterthought.
