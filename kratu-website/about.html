We started with a simple observation: every organisation has the data their people need. Almost none of them can actually use it. Kratu changes that.

The problem is always the same.

Whether it's a hospital in Nellore or a factory in Pune, the situation is identical: people who need answers, and data that already contains those answers.

The data exists in spreadsheets, PDFs, databases, and circulars. The person who needs it opens WhatsApp and asks a colleague who is also busy. Both people have now lost 15 minutes.

This happens thousands of times a day, in every organisation that employs more than 50 people. Kratu makes it stop.

Your data already exists

Student records in Firestore. SOPs in Drive. HR policies in a PDF from 2022. Leave records in Excel. The information is there.

Your people need answers

Every single day, your team asks questions that take 15 minutes and a phone call to answer. 90% of those questions have answers in your data.

Kratu closes the gap

Private AI that understands your data, respects your roles, answers in under 2 seconds, and cites exactly where the answer came from.

Why we started with engineering colleges.

The founding team at Saasta Software & Services has spent years working with engineering colleges across Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. We saw the same scene play out again and again.

A student goes to the office to ask about their backlog status. The admin is in a meeting. They WhatsApp a senior. The senior doesn't know. Someone calls the HOD's PA. The answer — which exists in a database — takes 2 days to reach the student.

"The answer existed. The student was 200 metres away from the database. There was no way to connect them without a human in the middle."

We built Kratu to be that connection. The first deployment was at an engineering college in AP. Students got their CGPA and backlog status instantly. Faculty got at-risk reports in under 2 seconds. The HOD got placement readiness dashboards for the first time.

We realised immediately: this works everywhere. Every hospital, factory, school, bank, and government office has the same gap. Kratu fills it.

How we build

Principles we don't compromise on.

🎯

Precision over recall

A wrong answer with high confidence is worse than no answer. Every Kratu response is cited, scored, and auditable. If the answer is unknown, we say so.

🔒

Privacy by architecture

Your data never touches a shared model. RBAC is enforced at the API layer, not the prompt layer. We designed for regulated industries from day one.

⚡

Speed is a feature

Sub-2s response times are non-negotiable. A system that takes 8 seconds to answer will never replace the habit of asking a colleague. Speed creates trust.

🌱

Deploy and improve

Observability is built in. Every failed answer is an improvement task. Every query is signal. We believe AI gets better with use, not just training.

🇮🇳

Built for India, built for everyone

We started in Andhra Pradesh. The constraints of the Indian enterprise market — price sensitivity, complex hierarchies, mixed data quality — made us better engineers.

🤝

The pilot is not a trial

When we offer a free 3-month pilot, we mean it. We set it up completely, integrate your data, and stay involved throughout. We win when you win.
