Questions about pricing.

Is the pilot really free?

Yes. No credit card, no hidden costs. We cover infrastructure, deployment, and support for 3 months. We do this because seeing Kratu answer real questions from your own data is worth more than any demo.

What counts as a "data source"?

Any distinct system: a CSV folder, a Firestore collection, a MySQL database, an API endpoint, a PDF drive. Multiple files of the same type (e.g., 200 PDFs) count as one source.

Can I switch plans mid-contract?

Yes. Upgrade anytime. Downgrades take effect at next billing cycle. Enterprise customers can negotiate terms mid-year.

Do you charge per query?

No. Flat monthly pricing, unlimited queries. We believe metered query pricing punishes you for success. You should never hesitate to let your team use the AI.

What if I have multiple campuses / offices?

The Institution plan covers one site. For multi-site (e.g., university system with 3 campuses, or a hospital with multiple branches), talk to us about Enterprise pricing.

Can I run it on our own servers?

Yes. On-premise and VPC deployment is an Enterprise feature. We support AWS, GCP, Azure, and bare metal. Your data literally never leaves your environment.
